## Graphic Designing Certification Courses + Live Projects

**Description:** This course covers essential skills for designing brochures, flyers, magazines, and books using popular software like Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop.

### Career Options:
- **DTP Operator**: Specializes in creating print-ready documents and publications.
- **Graphic Designer**: Works on a variety of visual content, from marketing materials to digital designs.
- **Print Designer**: Designs layouts specifically tailored for magazines, newspapers, and other print media.

### Who Can Take This Course?
- **Students**: Interested in publishing or print media.
- **Freelancers**: Who want to offer DTP services to local businesses.
- **Professionals**: In the advertising or publishing industry looking to sharpen their design skills.
